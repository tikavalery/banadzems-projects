About Me

I am a professional web developer with a passion for helping companies solve problems. My dream is to join your company and learn your stack so I can assist you in overcoming the challenges you might face. With this in mind, I have spent the past years developing the skills I need to demonstrate that I have what it takes to be an asset to you.

I have learned the MERN stack and have specialized in front-end web development, specifically in React.js, where I have built numerous projects as can be seen above. Building these projects, as well as completing a six-month bootcamp and numerous tutorials on Udemy, has given me a good understanding of how programming works.

My main goal when you hire me will be to first master your current stack as I have mastered the MERN stack, and then prepare myself to learn any new stack as needed.

I am also a great team player and communicator. Some of my projects were built in teams, like the bricks game, which was a project from my six-month bootcamp that I completed in San Francisco. I have a master's degree from the University of Wisconsin, where I learned a lot about working in teams as we did projects nearly on a bi-weekly basis in teams.

I intend to use these skills to be of value to your enterprise, and where I may need to amend my team playing and communication skills, I intend to read more books or take courses to improve. Thank you, and I look forward to hearing from you.
